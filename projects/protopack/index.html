Product Design 2022

ProtoPack

Scope

Physical product

Focus

Hands-on learning

ProtoPack imagines a backpack as an invitation to learn through making, exploring, and carrying ideas into the real world. The concept focuses on turning an everyday object into something more tactile, curious, and creatively enabling.

Overview

Quick read

Problem

Many learning products feel instructional first and inspiring second, leaving little room for curiosity or self-directed exploration.

Approach

Explored a backpack concept that supports making, carrying, and interacting with tools in a more playful way.

Outcome

The result is a product direction that connects mobility, creativity, and hands-on learning more deliberately.

Learning object

The backpack was treated as more than storage; it became part of the learning experience itself.

Curiosity first

The concept aims to make exploration feel inviting instead of overly instructional.

Tactile value

Physical interaction and form were central to how the idea communicates itself.

Context

A learning product becomes more memorable when it invites exploration before explanation

ProtoPack was shaped around the idea that tools can encourage curiosity through form, access, and interaction. The design direction focuses on making the product feel useful, distinctive, and creatively charged.
